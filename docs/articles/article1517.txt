Title: 9 Fast-Food Restaurants That Serve Healthy Foods

While most fast foods are based on cheap, unhealthy ingredients, many fast-food establishments now offer nutritious entrees and sides. Popular examples include wraps, salads, and burritos.
While most fast foods are based on cheap, unhealthy ingredients, many fast-food establishments now offer healthy entrees and accouterments.
The restaurants below are renowned for their wide selection of unhealthy foods. But if you don’t like indulging in traditional fast food, it’s good to know that they also serve healthier alternatives.
Although a few of these restaurants are only found in the United States, most are international.

Chipotle
Chipotle Mexican Grill is a restaurant chain that specializes in tacos and burritos. The  company uses  only organic, local ingredients and meats from naturally raised animals.
Healthier options:  burritos, tacos, and salads with a variety of meats, veggies, rice,  beans , and guacamole
Locations:  all over the United States, as well as Canada, France, Germany, and the United Kingdom
You can take a look at their menu  here .

Chick-fil-A
Chick-fil-A  is a fast-food restaurant that specializes in chicken sandwiches. It was one of the first chains in the United States to offer a menu completely free of  trans fat .
They have taken strides to make their dishes more healthy. In particular, their kids’ menu offers fruit cup sides and  milk  to drink.
Healthier  options:  grilled chicken nuggets, chicken salads, grilled market salad, and Greek yogurt parfait
Locations:  throughout the United States
You can take a look at their menu  here .

Wendy’s
Wendy’s is the third-largest hamburger fast-food chain in the world, behind McDonald’s and Burger King.
Although their menu consists mostly of hamburgers, chicken sandwiches, and  French fries , they also offer some healthier alternatives.
Healthier  options:   chicken salads  and chicken wraps
Locations:  30 countries worldwide, mostly in North and South America, Asia, and the Caribbean
You can take a look at their healthy salads  here .

McDonald’s
McDonald’s is the world’s biggest fast-food chain. It’s famous for its signature burgers, such as the Big Mac.
Most of the offerings at McDonald’s are highly processed and unhealthy. The company has been slammed for its role in facilitating the worldwide obesity and type 2 diabetes epidemics.
However, due partly to this critique, McDonald’s has added several healthy options to its menu.
Healthier  options:  several healthy salads, mostly made with chicken,  vegetables , and fruit
Locations:  worldwide, in a total of 119 countries
You can take a look at their salad menu  here .

Ruby Tuesday
Ruby Tuesday has many restaurants worldwide. Its menu offers a variety of foods, from steaks and seafood to pasta and salads.
The healthier options on their menu are under “Garden Bar & Fresh Salads.” Those dishes contain fewer than 700 calories per serving.
Ruby Tuesday offers options based on various dietary needs, such as  gluten intolerance .
Healthier  options:  a variety of meat, fish, and salads, with side dishes like baked  potatoes  and fresh vegetables
Locations:  North America and Europe
You can take a look at their menu  here .

The Cheesecake Factory
The Cheesecake Factory is known for gargantuan portion sizes and high  calorie  content.
However, due to increased pressure, they now offer a “SkinnyLicious®” menu with smaller portions and healthier ingredients.
Healthier  options:  steaks,  fish , seafood, salads, and various appetizers
Locations:  the United States, with several restaurants in the Middle East
You can take a look at their SkinnyLicious menu  here .

Kentucky Fried Chicken (KFC)
KFC is an international fast-food restaurant known for its signature  deep-fried  chicken. This franchise serves mainly unhealthy foods.
However, some of their restaurants sell grilled chicken to serve their more health-conscious customers.
Healthier  options:  sweet  corn  and mashed potatoes without gravy
Locations:  Worldwide
You can take a look at their menu  here .

Subway
Subway  is a fast-food restaurant primarily selling build-it-yourself submarine sandwiches (subs) and salads.
You can skip the bread altogether and get a salad with  chicken  breast and plenty of fresh vegetables.
Healthier  options:   whole-grain  bread, plus plenty of vegetables
Locations:  44,000 restaurants in 110 countries
You can take a look at their menu  here .

Panera Bread
Panera Bread  is a bakery, café, and restaurant that serves baked goods, soups, salads, and sandwiches.
Healthier  options:  a variety of soups and salads
Locations:  North America
You can take a look at their menu  here .

The bottom line
These days, almost every fast-food restaurant offers healthy options. If not, the meals can sometimes be modified to suit your dietary preferences or restrictions.
Some people prefer whole, protein-rich foods, such as steak or fish, along with a side of veggies and perhaps a baked potato. Fast-food restaurants usually have vegetarian options as well.
Take your time reading through the menu. You’ll most likely find something healthy—or something that can be made healthier with simple modifications.
