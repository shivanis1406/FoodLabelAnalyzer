Many brands now offer smoothie deliveries, making it easier than ever to enjoy fresh smoothies at home with minimal time or effort required.
However, these services can vary quite a bit, especially in terms of the quality, price, and amount of preparation needed.

How to choose a smoothie delivery service
There are a few factors to consider when selecting a smoothie delivery service that’s right for you.
For starters, be sure to check the ingredients list carefully and steer clear of smoothies high in added sugar and artificial flavors, additives, and preservatives.
You should also take a look at the nutrition label and look for a product that’s rich in important nutrients like  fiber , protein, vitamins, and minerals.
Ideally, companies should provide a wide range of smoothies, with a variety of flavors and ingredients to ensure that you’re getting plenty of nutrients in your diet.
If you have any dietary restrictions or allergies, you should also look for a company that caters to your specific needs.
Finally, be sure to consider the amount of preparation required for smoothies. While some may be fully prepared and ready to drink, others may need to be blended, shaken, or mixed before consumption.

What are the benefits of drinking smoothies?
Smoothies can be a great way to help squeeze a few extra servings of fruits and vegetables into your daily diet.
Studies show that eating more  fruits and vegetables  may be linked to a lower risk of cancer, heart disease, and premature death ( 1 ,  2) .
Drinking smoothies can also easily bump up your intake of fiber, an important nutrient that maintains digestive health, promotes regularity, stabilizes blood sugar levels, and supports weight management ( 3) .
Some also contain high protein ingredients like  protein powder , which may be especially beneficial if you’re trying to lose weight or maintain a moderate weight ( 4 ).
Finally, smoothies are convenient, require minimal preparation, and are easy to adapt to your personal needs and preferences.
