About Rybelsus
Rybelsus is a daily medication you’ll take as a tablet of 3 mg, 7 mg, or 14 mg. It contains the same active ingredient,  semaglutide , as  Ozempic  and is also approved for managing type 2 diabetes. However, clinicians may prescribe it off-label for weight loss. Off-label means it’s used for purposes not officially approved by regulators.
Research shows that Rybelsus is effective in type 2 diabetes management and may also help people lose weight.
In the  Pioneer 1 study , participants took daily oral semaglutide at doses of 3 mg, 7 mg, or 14 mg, or a placebo. After 6 months, those taking the semaglutide had average HbA1c reductions of 0.6% to 1.1%, with bodyweight reduction of around 5 pounds (about 2.3 kilograms) for the 14-mg dose.
