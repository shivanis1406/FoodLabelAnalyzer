Title: Southwestern Corn Hash

Southwestern Corn Hash
4 cobs of fresh corn, kernels cut from the cob
1 small onion, chopped fine
1 red pepper, chopped
1 tablespoon olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste
Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Lay out all the vegetables on a baking sheet in a single layer. Drizzle with the olive oil and toss evenly to coat.
Roast for approximately 20 minutes until the edges of the corn are lightly browned. Season and serve.
