Combine all ingredients together. Marinate for 4 hours. Grill chicken on one side and finish in a 350 degrees Fahrenheit oven, until chicken reaches internal temp of 165 degrees Fahrenheit.
Rub tomatoes and garlic with olive oil. Roast tomatoes in a 325 degrees Fahrenheit oven for 30 minutes and roast garlic in 325 degrees Fahrenheit oven for 15 minutes. Pur√©e tomatoes and garlic and fold into remaining ingredients.
Spoon beans onto plate, top with chicken breast, and sprinkle with fresh chopped basil.
