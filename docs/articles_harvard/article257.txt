Title: Asian Diabetes Prevention Initiative

The Asian Diabetes Prevention Initiative (ADPI) aims to create and spread an effective public health message to help counter the rising rates of diabetes among Asian populations. It is a joint collaboration between the Harvard T.H. Chan School of Public Health, Department of Nutrition and the National University of Singapore, Saw Swee Hock School of Public Health.
