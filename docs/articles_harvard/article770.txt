Title: Yellow Squash with Sage

Yellow Squash with Sage
4 small yellow squash, or 3 medium
1 to 2 tablespoons olive oil
2 teaspoons fresh sage, chopped fine
Salt and pepper to taste
Slice squash into disks about 1/8 inch thick. Warm 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a medium-sized saut√© pan over medium heat. Add squash, turning frequently, and cook until just tender. If squash dries out while cooking, drizzle with remaining tablespoon of olive oil; toss to coat.
Sprinkle with sage, salt and pepper to taste, and toss.
